Who am I?

As a career path, I am a college student studying Computer Science. Blah blah

My life as a hobbyist is a lot more interesting. I have played guitar for around 8 years. God has blessed me with the opportunity to be a worship leader both at my church as well as at my college Christian ministry. In recent years, I have found myself with a thirst for knowledge that has led me down some interesting avenues. Musically, I have learned how to play several new instruments, including the hammered dulcimer and, of course, the bowed psaltery. God has also blessed me with a few original songs.

I have also exlplored other venues, having found myself with a certain fascination for archaic weaponry. This ultimately led me to an interest in bullwhips (which are really a tool, not a weapon; but who doesn't to wield a whip like Indy Jones?). Now I build and sell custom bullwhips and snakewhips.
