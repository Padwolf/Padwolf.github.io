Usage Rights

All my songs are a gift from God. My desire is for them to be used for His glory. In light of this, you are more than welcome to play my songs for any free or not-for-profit event. Examples of free events are church service worship or worship gatheries (i.e. a worship night). Events that, for the purposes of my songs, are considered "not-for-profit" are events that charge customers but the charges are to cover the cost of the event or surpluses are given to a specific cause. An example of the former is a worship night in a rented facility where an entry fee is charged to cover the cost of renting the facility. An example of the latter is a fundraiser event.

In all of the above and all following scenarios, I do request that you give credit where credit is due. Please credit me as the writer of the songs.

There are a few scenarios where I explicitly request you contact me. The first instance is if you would like to use one of my songs in an event where you expect to earn a profit. Also, if you would like to use my song in a large event (weekly worship services at a large church excepted) or would like to do a cover, please contact me. In the latter two cases, is very unlikely that I will ask for any payment, but I would like to know.

To contact me, send me an email at padwolf99@gmail.com.
Be sure the subject line is relevant, otherwise I may overlook your email.
