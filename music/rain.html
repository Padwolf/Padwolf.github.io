Background

Maybe it's because I've grown up in a desert, but I have always loved the rain. There is something about the atmosphere around a rain storm that is just... awesome. When I'm in the midst of rain, I'm always reminded of God and of His power. My God is so powerful! He is the God who can call down fire from the heavens and hold apart the waters of a sea. He can raise the dead, and create the entirety of space - every star, every planet, and everything else - in one day! And through His love and by His grace, He chose to use His awesome power to mend the otherwise irreparable chasm between us, so that we may forever have a relationship with Him.
Our God is so powerful, indeed infinitely powerful, yet has complete control over His power. When I see the rain, I see an example of the "gentle power" of God. We often take for granted the power in the rain. Water has a lot of destructive potential. Water carves out canyons, rusts metal, and floods towns. But more often than not, especially where I live, the rain isn't a crashing, destructive force, but a gentle, healing force. There is something about the patter of rain and the distant roll of thunder that demonstrates the incredible power of the rain. Yet when I hear it, it doesn't fill me with fear but with peace. That is the power of God. Such tremendous power, and we fully deserve the wrath of God. But we can find rest and peace in His power, knowing that God is a refuge for those who seek Him.
I prayed that God would give me a song about rain for months before God gave me this. May you glorify Him because of it.
