Background

This is one of the first songs I wrote, and the first of mine I performed live. This song was written during a really stressful time in my life. Amongst other things, I was not happy how we had been leading our robotics team, and it was a week before our second competition. I also had more assignments than I had time for. This song was a reflection of the chaos I felt, but I knew that I had to keep trusting God, because He would work everything out. That week God pulled through in some mighty ways.
Whenever things get hard or stressful, I know I can turn to God. He holds everything in His hands, and He will pull me through. And even if things don't go as I expect, I can still trust in His plan. It's awesome to know that the God of the Universe has my back.


Lyrics

Verse 1

My attention a million things demand
To stay afloat in this chaotic land
Should I ignore them, they threaten to make
My weary, weathered spirit break
But you tell me to worry not
My well-being is Your ultimate thought
My success is Your desire
You want only to lift me higher


Chorus

I will trust that Your words are true
That You're guiding me to make it through
That You're leading me to a better place
And you lavish me with unending grace


Verse 2

There are things for which I often pray
But they're unanswered to this day
I wonder when You'll come through
Or if You simply will refuse
But in Your Word You say to wait
Though it may seem to have doomed fate
My prayers You'll answer when the time is right
And You'll come through with Your endless might


Bridge

The enemy is pressing me
I'm crushed under everything
From the sea save me, I plea
Before I drown in the Entropy


Writer Commentary

There are some lyrics in the first verse ("My well-being" and following lines) that could be misconstrued as Prosperity Gospel. These lyrics were written considering spiritual success (furthering God's kingdom and spiritual growth), not earthly success (wealth, status, happiness, etc.).
