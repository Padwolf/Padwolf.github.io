Background

Have you ever stopped to ponder how wonderous the sky is? In a single day, it can take on every color of the rainbow and then some as the clouds sit almost magically on an invisible plane. It's such a complex canvas of beauty, but we rarely stop to admire it. When I think how this incredibly complex entity is but a very tiny fragment of God's creation, I'm bewildered by the depth of His imagination.
But of all God's creations, we are His masterpiece. This statement seems to be a paradox; The one part of creation that is inherently flawed because of the corruption of sin is the part that God considers the best. God loves you and me more than He ever loved any other facet of creation. He loves us so much that He gave up heaven, a sacrifice we will never understand in this life, and chose to walk the earth without a home, and ultimately suffer one of the most horrendous deaths that ever occurred. I believe that He would do it all over again even if only one person received the choice of salvation.
