Heads-Up Display

Have you ever wanted to have a navigation system in front of you while you drive, but still be able to see the road? Or have you ever wanted to watch a video or show but be able to see past the screen? Or have you ever wanted to amaze your friends with a cool scientific gadget? Well, here's your chance. Using an old device with a screen, a bit of transparent plastic, and perhaps a mirror, you can make your own transparent or heads-up display.
