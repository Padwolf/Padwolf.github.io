Welcome!
We are high school seniors at nex+Gen Academy. We have been assigned a project that has to deal with recycling, our group specifically got assigned repurposing. As a group, we created a website showing how to reuse common household items that are typically thrown away. We are doing this because waste is getting to be a huge part of our daily lives: our waste is harmful to animals, and our waste is getting on the streets making the place we live look trashy (no pun intended ;)). You may not see it clearly yet, but it is affecting our environment and if we don't change our ways now it could really harm us in the future.
